OTTAWA CHINESE TABLE TENNIS CLUB / 渥太華中華乒乓球會

Established in 1983, the Ottawa Chinese Table Tennis Club, located at 2120 Prince Charles Road in Ottawa, Ontario, is a destination for table tennis enthusiasts. Originally founded as a cultural hub for the Chinese community, the club proudly embraces players from all backgrounds, fostering a diverse and inclusive environment. This non-profit, volunteer-run organization not only fosters a diverse and inclusive environment but also stands as a beacon of sportsmanship and unity in Canada's capital.

渥太华华人乒乓球俱乐部成立于 1983 年，位于安大略省渥太华市查尔斯王子路 2120 号，是乒乓球爱好者的胜地。该俱乐部最初是作为华人社区的文化中心而成立的，现在它自豪地接纳来自各种背景的球员，营造了一个多元化的包容性环境。这个由志愿者运营的非营利组织不仅营造了一个多元化的包容性环境，还成为加拿大首都体育精神和团结的灯塔。
